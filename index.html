"Exploring Effective Speech Representation via ASR for High-Quality End-to-End Multispeaker TTS"


Authors:Kaili,Zhang†, Cheng Gong†
Abstract:Style modelling is an important issue in expressive neuraltext-to-speech. Specifically, the style vector is extracted fromthe style encoder, the text vector is extracted from the text en-coder, and then style vector concatenated with text encoder asthe input of the decoder to synthesize the audio of the desiredstyle. However, the style vector extracted by the style encoderwill entangle some content information, and it will cause conflicts with the real input content, which will have a negativeimpact on the synthesized audio. In this work, we added theASR task, and did not let the task of ASR learn well, in order to remove the content information before the style vectoris extracted by the style encoder. We use objective evalua-tion word error rate (WER) to prove that our method can efectively alleviate the problem of entangle between style andcontent information. The subjective evaluation of ABX alsoshows that our method can also maintain a better style transferability.

Definition (same as defined in our submitted paper)

Ground Truth: Audios selected from Blizzard Challenge 2013 testset.
Baseline Modified Tacotron Model: It is our pre-trained TTS model, which is an extension on the tacotron model by adding a reference encoder and a share layer.
Baseline ASR_guide Introducing the ASR model, and fine-tune the TTS model under the guidance of the ASR model.
Our proposed We add the pre-trained ASR model to the pre-trained modified tacotron model, and then continue to train the joint modified tacotron model, and keep the parameters of the ASR model fixed during the training process
